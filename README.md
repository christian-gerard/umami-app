## AI Recipe Generator

Have you ever come home one night and realized, "I forgot to get groceries!" Its already late and you don't want to go back to the store. You scrounge for leftovers but you're not sure what you could make. Thats where Umami comes in. Input the ingredients you have on hand and let it propose a recipe idea for your meal.

## User Interface

### User Login / SignUp

<img width="824" alt="Screen Shot 2024-05-09 at 11 46 36 PM" src="https://github.com/christian-gerard/umami/assets/102694030/aa8daf60-1a79-48a2-9e40-e67fb92f5bb9">

Upon visiting Umami you will be able to either login to a prexisting account or create a new account. Account creation requires the following fields...

- Username
- Password
- Email

Once these are entered you will be able to access the Umami application.

### Cookbook

A user's cookbook is where all a user can interact with all of their saved recipes.

<img width="1717" alt="Screen Shot 2024-05-10 at 12 29 33 AM" src="https://github.com/christian-gerard/umami/assets/102694030/6510554d-34a4-4a3d-a08b-1abd4890d85c">

Each recipe previews 4 fields when viewing multiple recipes at once. Most of thee fields are summaries of the full information and we will give a detailed explanation of each later on. You are able to add a new recipe here by navigating to the upper right hand and clicking on 'New Recipe+'. This opens up a form in which you are able to add recipe information...

<img width="1203" alt="Screen Shot 2024-05-10 at 12 40 33 AM" src="https://github.com/christian-gerard/umami/assets/102694030/c4b495f5-ae92-4aac-addd-79b173e699c0">

Required inputs are Recipe Name, Category, Prep Time, Source, and Instructions. A recipe can be added without adding ingredients or an image. If not image is uploaded then umami will default to an image of a red top mushroom.

Once you have specified your recipe details you can click on 'Add Recipe' to create the recipe and be navigated back to the cookbook page.

### Recipes

To view individual recipes click on a recipe card. This will navigate you to a recipe view where you are able to view all of the recipe details in full. If you are looking edit or delete the recipe this can be done here as well.
